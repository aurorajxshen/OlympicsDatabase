Olympic Colours

The Olympic colours convey the spirit of the Olympic Games through their specific colour palette. Use them individually and in combinations to build your own imagery that is instantly recognizable and unique. Colours were first used for communication in the 1960s for the Summer Olympic Games and since Sarajevo 1984 at the Winter Olympic Games.
